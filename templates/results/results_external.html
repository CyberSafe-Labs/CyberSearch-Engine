We know that some sites are better at finding certain information than others. By clicking one of these links we will redirect you to the site specified and automatically run your search term through their search engine!
Be careful! We cannot ensure that these sites hold your privacy as dear as we do. Click at your own risk!
You can also add and remove external links using this control panel. To add, we just need a search link such as "https://www.infinitysearchsolo.com/results?q=" to which we can append your search term to. A text label and a link to an icon are also useful to help you know where an external link will take you.
