About CyberSearch

What We Do

CyberSearch is an open source metasearch engine with a special focus on privacy and efficiency. All it really does is proxies results from Bing and Wikipedia to you with a customizable user interface.

How CyberSearch Works

When you search on our site, we take results from Bing and Wikipedia, organize them, and display them directly to you without logging any identifiable information about you.

How We Make Money

CyberSearch doesn't make any money.

About Our Company

CyberSearch is part of CyberSafe Labs, Inc. and we are just a small team working to improve softwares.
